Color Field & Mixed Media

Mateo
Silva

Born in Guadalajara, Mateo Silva builds luminous compositions that explore emotion through color, rhythm, and layered transparency. Her practice moves between painting and mixed media, creating immersive surfaces that suggest states of contemplation, inner movement, and sensorial memory.

"Color is not decoration; it is the emotional structure of the piece."
